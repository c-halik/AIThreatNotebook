# Welcome to your AI security notebook

This is your personal notebook. Anything you write here (as `.md` files in
`knowledge_base/notebook/`) gets chunked, embedded, and made searchable by
the chatbot after you click **Rebuild index**.

Use it for:
- Notes on CVEs, papers, or talks you've read about AI/agentic AI security
- Your own threat models for the agents/tools you're building
- Runbooks for your cloud lab environment

Reference material (PDFs, longer docs) goes in `knowledge_base/docs/`
instead, either by copying files there directly or uploading them from the
Knowledge Base tab in the app.
